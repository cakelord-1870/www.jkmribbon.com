Morex Fabric Velvet Ribbon (Nylvalour) - 2" ; 11 Yards

Morex

Regular price $41.25

Our fabric velvet ribbon, nylvalour, comes in 71 beautiful colors and the following widths and put-ups: 3/16" width on 11 & 219 yard spools (200 meters); 5/16" width on 11 & 164 yard spools (150 meters); 3/8" width on 11 & 164 yard spools (150 meters); 5/8" width on 11 & 109 yard spools (100 meters); 7/8" width on 11 & 82 yard spools (75 meters); 1-1/2" width on 11 & 55 yard spools (50 meters) and 2" width on 11 & 55 yard spools (50 meters). This high quality velvet is made in Switzerland. It is colorsafe, so it works beautifully on garments. This velvet ribbon is also great for jewelry design, invitations, floral design, and gift wrap. The specifications we have are as follow: 1. 100% Nylon. Colorfast under the following conditions. 2. Machine washable up to 104 degrees Farenheit. (cool or warm only) 3. No Chlorine treatment. 4. No Hot Iron, cool only. 5. Dry Cleanable 6. Do not put in Dryer. Made only with the finest quality materials in Switzerland.
